---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
muon_propagation_WGAN_model.h5
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Wasserstein GAN with Gradient Penalty (WGAN-GP) for muon propagation through pipe
Conditioned to pipe radius (as one-hot encoded vector)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Training data: Pipe samples with radius 4,6,8,10,14,16,18,20 mm
	Aiming for interpolation to 12 mm pipe

Preprocessing: scaling first and second detector kinematic variables, 
		using sample_weight = 1 / sqrt(Deltax^2 + Deltay^2)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Architecture:

Critic (C):	Input: 4 + (4 + 8)
		Dense 128 LeakyReLU 0.2
                Dense 64  LeakyReLU 0.2
                Dense 32  LeakyReLU 0.2
                Output 1  Linear

Generator (G):	Input: 16 + (4 + 8)
                Dense 32  LeakyReLU 0.2
                Dense 64  LeakyReLU 0.2
                Dense 128 LeakyReLU 0.2
		Output 4  Linear
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Training options:
		Total number of samples = ~5000000
		Batch size = 5000
		Learning rate = 0.0001 (Adam)
		Epochs = 1000
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Evaluation:
		300000 samples were generated for each training radius
		and also 300000 samples of radius 12mm using an interpolated label
